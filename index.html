About KESSI

Founded in March 2, 2006, KingEdward Security Services, Inc. (KESSI) is a Philippine private security agency that provides security services to private and government establishments nationwide. The organization emphasizes professional standards and continuous growth since its inception.

Mission
Provide total security protection of lives and properties through reliable, disciplined and well-trained security personnel, supported by continuous training and proper equipment.

Vision & Core Values
Vision: To become one of the best security service providers in the Philippines — recognized for standard quality performance and professionalism.
Core Values: Kindness, Efficiency, Security & Safety, Service, and Integrity — supported by a culture of excellence, learning and adaptability.
