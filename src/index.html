Our agents undergo rigorous, continuous training programs that exceed industry standards. We cover tactical skills, de-escalation techniques, emergency medical response, and advanced surveillance technology to ensure our team is prepared for any scenario.

We seek candidates with a background in military, law enforcement, or a related field. A high level of physical fitness, impeccable integrity, and the ability to operate under pressure are essential. All applicants must pass a comprehensive background check and psychological evaluation.
