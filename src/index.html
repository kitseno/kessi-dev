About KESSI

Founded March 2, 2006, KingEdward Security Services, Inc. (KESSI) is a Philippine private security agency that provides security services to private and government establishments nationwide. The organization emphasizes professional standards and continuous growth since inception.

Mission

Provide total security protection of lives and properties through reliable, disciplined and well-trained security personnel, supported by continuous training and proper equipment.

Vision and Core Values

Vision: To become one of the best security service providers in the Philippines — recognized for standard quality performance and professionalism.

Core Values: Kindness, Efficiency, Security & Safety, Service, and Integrity — supported by a culture of excellence, learning and adaptability.

Our Training Programs

Our security personnel undergo rigorous, continuous training programs that exceed industry standards. We cover tactical skills, de-escalation techniques, emergency medical response, and advanced surveillance technology to ensure our team is prepared for any scenario.

Candidate Requirements

We seek candidates with a background in military, law enforcement, or a related field. A high level of physical fitness, impeccable integrity, and the ability to operate under pressure are essential. All applicants must pass a comprehensive background check and psychological evaluation.
